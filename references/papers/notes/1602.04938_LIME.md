## 1. Key Innovation & Algorithmic Breakthrough

Introduces Local Interpretable Model-agnostic Explanations (LIME), fitting sparse linear surrogate models around perturbed local samples.

---

## 2. Core Mathematical Formulations

$$\text{explanation}(x) = \arg\min_{g \in G} \mathcal{L}(f, g, \pi_x) + \Omega(g)$$

---

## 3. Architecture & Operational Mechanics

Explains predictions of any black-box classifier locally.
