## 1. Key Innovation & Algorithmic Breakthrough

Uses Reinforcement Learning from Human Feedback (RLHF) with Proximal Policy Optimization (PPO) to align language models with user intent.

---

## 2. Core Mathematical Formulations

$$\text{obj}(\theta) = \mathbb{E}_{(x,y)} \left[ r_\psi(x,y) - \beta D_{\text{KL}}(\pi_\theta(y|x) || \pi_{\text{SFT}}(y|x)) \right]$$

## 3. Architecture & Operational Mechanics

Three-stage pipeline: Supervised Fine-Tuning (SFT), Reward Model Training ($r_\psi$), and PPO Reinforcement Learning Policy optimization.
