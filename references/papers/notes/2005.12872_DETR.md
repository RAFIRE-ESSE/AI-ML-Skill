## 1. Key Innovation & Algorithmic Breakthrough

Redefines object detection as a direct set prediction problem using Transformers and bipartite Hungarian matching loss.

---

## 2. Core Mathematical Formulations

$$\mathcal{L}_{Hungarian}(y, \hat{y}) = \sum_{i=1}^N \left[ -\log \hat{p}_{\sigma(i)}(c_i) + \mathcal{L}_{box}(b_i, \hat{b}_{\sigma(i)}) \right]$$

---

## 3. Architecture & Operational Mechanics

Eliminates hand-crafted anchor boxes, region proposals, and Non-Maximum Suppression (NMS).
