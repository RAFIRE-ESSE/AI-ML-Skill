## 1. Key Innovation & Algorithmic Breakthrough

Prepends trainable continuous task-specific vectors (prefixes) to Transformer key and value layers while keeping all PLM weights frozen.

---

## 2. Core Mathematical Formulations

$$h_i = \begin{cases} P_K[i, :] & \text{if } i < |P| \\ \text{LM}(x_i, h_{<i}) & \text{otherwise} \end{cases}$$

## 3. Architecture & Operational Mechanics

Learns a small prefix MLP reparameterization matrix during training, requiring 0.1% parameters.
