## 1. Key Innovation & Algorithmic Breakthrough

Formulates generative diffusion models using parameterized reverse Markov chains to denoise Gaussian noise step-by-step.

---

## 2. Core Mathematical Formulations

$$\mathcal{L}_{simple}(\theta) = \mathbb{E}_{t, x_0, \epsilon} \left[ \|\epsilon - \epsilon_a(x_t, t)\|^2 \right]$$

---

## 3. Architecture & Operational Mechanics

Trains U-Net networks to predict injected noise $\epsilon$ at discrete timestep $t$.
