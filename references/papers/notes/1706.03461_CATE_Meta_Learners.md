## 1. Key Innovation & Algorithmic Breakthrough

Formalizes meta-learner architecture frameworks (S-Learner, T-Learner, X-Learner) for heterogeneous treatment effect estimation.

---

## 2. Core Mathematical Formulations

$$\text{X-Learner}: D^{(1)} = Y^{(1)} - \hat{\mu}_0(X^{(1)}), \quad D^{(0)} = \hat{\mu}_1(X^{(0)}) - Y^{(0)}$$

---

## 3. Architecture & Operational Mechanics

Superior CATE estimation when control and treatment group sizes are heavily imbalanced.
