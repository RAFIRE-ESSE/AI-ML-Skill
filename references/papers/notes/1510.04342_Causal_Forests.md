## 1. Key Innovation & Algorithmic Breakthrough

Introduces Causal Forests, adapting random decision trees to estimate Heterogeneous Treatment Effects (CATE) $\tau(x)$ non-parametrically.

---

## 2. Core Mathematical Formulations

$$\hat{\tau}(x) = \sum_{i=1}^n W_i(x) (Y_i^{(1)} - Y_i^{(0)})$$

---

## 3. Architecture & Operational Mechanics

Honest splitting protocol ensuring unbiased confidence interval estimation for personalized treatment effects.
