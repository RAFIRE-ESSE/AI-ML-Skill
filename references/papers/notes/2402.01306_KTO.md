## 1. Key Innovation & Algorithmic Breakthrough

Formulates model alignment directly from binary loss/gain signals (like/dislike) using Prospect Theory without requiring paired preferences.

---

## 2. Core Mathematical Formulations

$$\mathcal{L}_{KTO}(\pi_\theta, \pi_{\text{ref}}) = \lambda_y \sigma\left( \beta \log \frac{\pi_\theta(y|x)}{\pi_{\text{ref}}(y|x)} - z_{\text{ref}} \right)$$

---

## 3. Architecture & Operational Mechanics

Eliminates preference-pair collection bottleneck by directly optimizing binary feedback.
