## 1. Key Innovation & Algorithmic Breakthrough

Introduces Zero Redundancy Optimizer (ZeRO), sharding optimizer states (ZeRO-1), gradients (ZeRO-2), and parameters (ZeRO-3) across data-parallel GPUs.

---

## 2. Core Mathematical Formulations
