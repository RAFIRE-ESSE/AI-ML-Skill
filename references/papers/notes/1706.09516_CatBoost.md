## 1. Key Innovation & Algorithmic Breakthrough

Introduces CatBoost gradient boosting framework featuring ordered target encoding and symmetric oblivious decision trees.

---

## 2. Core Mathematical Formulations

$$\hat{x}_k^i = \frac{\sum_{j \in M_i} y_j + a \cdot p}{|M_i| + a}$$

---

## 3. Architecture & Operational Mechanics

Prevents target leakage natively while eliminating manual categorical encoding preprocessing.
