## 1. Key Innovation & Algorithmic Breakthrough

Scales sparse Mixture-of-Experts (MoE) models by routing each token to a single top-1 expert, cutting compute while scaling parameters 10x+.

---

## 2. Core Mathematical Formulations

$$p_i(x) = \text{Softmax}(\text{Top1}(\text{Router}(x)))$$

---

## 3. Architecture & Operational Mechanics

Replaces standard FFN layers with a routing matrix that routes tokens to specialized expert sub-networks with capacity factor balancing.
