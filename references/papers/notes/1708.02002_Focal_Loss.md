## Key Innovation

Identifies extreme background-foreground class imbalance (1:1000) as the primary bottleneck holding back single-stage object detectors (e.g., RetinaNet). Introduces **Focal Loss**, which dynamically down-weights easy background samples during training, ensuring gradients focus on hard misclassified candidates.

---

## Loss Formulation

$$\text{FL}(p_t) = -\alpha_t (1 - p_t)^\gamma \log(p_t)$$

- $p_t$: Model's estimated probability for the ground-truth class.
- $(1 - p_t)^\gamma$: Modulating factor with focusing parameter $\gamma \ge 0$.
  - When $p_t \to 1$ (easy sample), $(1 - p_t)^\gamma \to 0$, rendering loss weight negligible.
  - When $p_t \to 0$ (hard misclassified sample), $(1 - p_t)^\gamma \to 1$, preserving full loss gradient.
- $\alpha_t$: Balanced alpha parameter for weighting positive vs negative classes.
