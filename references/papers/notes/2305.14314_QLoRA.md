- **Venue/Year**: 2023 (NeurIPS 2023)

## 1. Key Innovation & Algorithmic Breakthrough

Introduces 4-bit NormalFloat (NF4) quantization, Double Quantization, and Paged Optimizers to enable fine-tuning 70B parameter LLMs on 24GB GPUs.

---

## 2. Core Mathematical Formulations

$$q_{NF4}(x) = \frac{1}{2} \left( Q_X\left(i\right) + Q_X\left(i+1\right) \right)$$

---

## 3. Architecture & Operational Mechanics

Quantizes base weights to information-theoretically optimal 4-bit NormalFloat while backpropagating gradients through LoRA adapters.
