## 1. Key Innovation & Algorithmic Breakthrough

Demonstrates that scaling autoregressive language models to 175 billion parameters enables strong zero-shot, one-shot, and few-shot performance across tasks without task-specific fine-tuning.

## 2. Core Mathematical Formulations

$$\mathcal{L}(\theta) = -\sum_{i} \log P(x_i | x_1, \dots, x_{i-1}; \theta)$$
