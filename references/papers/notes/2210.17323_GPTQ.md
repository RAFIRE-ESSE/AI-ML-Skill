## 1. Key Innovation & Algorithmic Breakthrough

Executes fast 3-bit / 4-bit post-training weight quantization using second-order Taylor expansion error compensation.

---

## 2. Core Mathematical Formulations

$$w_q = \text{argmin}_{w_q} (w - w_q)^T H (w - w_q)$$

---

## 3. Architecture & Operational Mechanics

Quantizes 175B parameter models in hours with negligible loss in accuracy.
