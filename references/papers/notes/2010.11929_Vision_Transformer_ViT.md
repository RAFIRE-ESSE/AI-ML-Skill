## Key Innovation

Applies standard Transformer encoder architecture directly to image patch sequences with minimal modifications. Demonstrates that reliance on CNN translational equivariance and spatial locality inductive biases is unnecessary when pre-trained on massive datasets (JFT-300M / ImageNet-21k).

---

## Formulation

1. **Patch Extraction**: Image $x \in \mathbb{R}^{H \times W \times C}$ is reshaped into sequence of 2D patches $x_p \in \mathbb{R}^{N \times (P^2 \cdot C)}$, where $N = \frac{HW}{P^2}$ (e.g. $16 \times 16$).
2. **Linear Embedding**: Patches are mapped into dimension $D$ via linear projection:
   $$z_0 = [x_{\text{class}}; x_p^1 E; x_p^2 E; \dots; x_p^N E] + E_{\text{pos}}$$
3. **Transformer Processing**: Standard Multi-Head Self-Attention layers process tokens globally.
