## 1. Key Innovation & Algorithmic Breakthrough

Presents the Segment Anything Model (SAM), a foundation model for universal promptable image segmentation trained on 1.1 billion masks.

---

## 2. Core Mathematical Formulations

$$\text{Prompt Decoder}: \text{Cross-Attention}(\text{Prompt Tokens}, \text{Image Embedding})$$

---

## 3. Architecture & Operational Mechanics

Heavy ViT Image Encoder + Light Prompt Encoder (points, boxes, text) + Fast Transformer Mask Decoder.
