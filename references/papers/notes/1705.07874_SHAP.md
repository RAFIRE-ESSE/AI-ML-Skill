## 1. Key Innovation & Algorithmic Breakthrough

Unified Shapley Additive Explanations (SHAP) framework computing exact game-theoretic Shapley value attributions for complex models.

---

## 2. Core Mathematical Formulations

$$\phi_i(x) = \sum_{S \subseteq F \setminus \{i\}} \frac{|S|!(|F|-|S|-1)!}{|F|!} \left[ f_x(S \cup \{i\}) - f_x(S) \right]$$

---

## 3. Architecture & Operational Mechanics

TreeSHAP algorithm evaluates tree ensemble attributions in $O(TLD^2)$ polynomial time.
